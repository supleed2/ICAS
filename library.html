Manga Library

Our society library containing English and Japanese language Anime, Manga and Art Supplies

Anime & Manga Library

Our library contains a wide array of manga and anime from which members can borrow books and DVDs.

If you'd like to borrow from the library, email the society email (animesoc@imperial.ac.uk) with your name, CID and mobile number and of course what you'd like to borrow.

Our current inventory is listed here. If you are interested in purchasing some titles that are in the inventory, let us know on our society email (animesoc@imperial.ac.uk) as we may have copies and will be able to sell it cheaper than store value.

Art Materials

We have a slowly increasing collection of art materials including small and medium Wacom graphic tablets, markers, copics, and a scanner. A full list of materials is in the works. Please read the rules for borrowing and using markers and Wacom tablets.
